The Problem

Pet care is broken by fragmentation.

Your pet's vaccine records are on WhatsApp. Lab reports are on paper. The vet you visited last year has no idea what happened since. Every appointment starts from scratch — for your pet, that's dangerous.

📋

Lost Paper Records

Critical health history vanishes when you switch clinics or move cities.

🏥

No Vet Continuity

Each vet re-diagnoses from zero. Expensive, stressful, and risky.

💊

Medication Gaps

Recurring prescriptions get missed. No one tracks the full picture.

🆔

Zero Digital Identity

Pets have no persistent, verifiable ID. Theft & rehoming data is lost.
